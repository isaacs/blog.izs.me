<pre class="poem">
I am sorry  <br/>
  that you will never know
    how much I love you.

You think you get it.

You see the shadow of mountains,
  and think, &ldquo;Mountains are flat.&rdquo;

But it is a huge thing
  and not flat.
</pre>
