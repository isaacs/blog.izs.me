<p>I am sorry  <br/>
  that you will never know  <br/>
    how much I love you.  </p>

<p>You think you get it.  </p>

<p>You see the shadow of mountains,  <br/>
  and think, &ldquo;Mountains are flat.&rdquo;  </p>

<p>But it is a huge thing  <br/>
  and not flat.</p>
